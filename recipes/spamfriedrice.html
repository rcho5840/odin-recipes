Spam Fried Rice

Description

A simple yet effective meal that is a staple of any "Asian-child-with-parents-that-work household"

Ingredients

  1. Half a cube of Costco spam
  2. 3-4 Eggs
  3. A tablespoon of soy sauce
  4. 1.5 cups of cooked white rice
  5. OPTIONAL: Ketchup, Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut spam into cubes and cook on high heat
  2. At the same time, fry eggs in a seperate pan with pepper and cover lid
  3. While spam cooks, get your rice into bowl and put optional ketchup
  4. Once spam is almost done cooking, add soy sauce
  5. merge everything and enjoy!
